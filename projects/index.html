This Website:

I chose to make this website from scratch rather than use a pre-made template since I had no previous UI/UX design or frontend experience. Doing a project like this where I have no experience gives me more insight into coding (and appreciation of the work that goes into websites).
TailwindCSS was appealing as it gave me a higher level of control over how I chose to structure the site. Using a framework like this gives me a much deeper understanding of the steps to website design than frameworks like Bootstrap. It also allowed me to easily implement responsiveness and have it in mind during every iteration of the site.

I will continuously be updating and changing the website as I improve. The first changes will be adding Vue.JS to the website in the future as well as WebAssembly-based projects under the Resources tab.
